Connect Zalando to Billbee and unlock a new sales channel

Seller Partner is a complete solution for Billbee users to start selling in Zalando. Import orders, sync inventory, generate labels and documents and more. For Partner and ZFS orders.
Features Get Started

Seller Partner takes care of these for you

Import Zalando orders

Import orders into Billbee and submit their tracking and return numbers to Zalando when they're shipped. Support Partner (shipped by you) and ZFS (shipped by Zalando) orders. Support to all Euro marketplaces (all coming soon).

Sync inventory

Synchronize inventory between Billbee and Zalando. Sell only what you're ready to ship.

Generate documents

Generate Zalando-compliant delivery notes, return notes and invoices for all marketplaces. Avoid penalties due to non-compliant documents. Automatically send invoices by email (as required by each marketplace).

Generate product labels

Generate Zalando-compliant product and box labels. Avoid inventory relabelling due to non-compliance.

Automate refunds

Automatically create credit notes in Billbee for refunded orders in Zalando. Support Partner and ZFS orders.

Manage prices

Edit regular and promotional prices in a web interface (CSV only if you wish).

Upload to the cloud

Upload documents to your preferred cloud or printing service and automate your workflow (coming soon).

API Access

Connect other tools to Seller Partner via API for further automation (coming soon).
